=== DEMO: Without -fcheck=bounds (no bounds checking) ===

Program: demo_oob.f90
  allocate(A(5:15))   ! bounds: lb=5, ub=15
  A(5) = 99.0
  i = 20              ! out-of-bounds index
  print *, A(i)       ! silent OOB — no error, wrong value returned

Compile:
  $ flang demo_oob.f90 flang_bounds_check.o -o demo_unsafe

Run:
  $ ./demo_unsafe

Output:
  Accessing A(20) on array with bounds [5:15]...
  0.

--- The program silently returns garbage (0.) instead of aborting. ---
--- No warning. No indication that the access was out of bounds.   ---
